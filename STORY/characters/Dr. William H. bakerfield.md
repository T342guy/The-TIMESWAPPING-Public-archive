Dr. William henry bakerfield, also known as [^1]Dr. William H. bakerfield, is the founder of [[The Nova genesis foundation]], a quite bright and humble man, and a burning desire to make science better then what it was at the time. 

His partner in operation, and his good friend, anonymously named "[[Og]]", was a heavy influence to the history of the foundation.
