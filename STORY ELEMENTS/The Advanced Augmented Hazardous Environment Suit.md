#stryelem 
The Augmented Hazardous Environment Suit, also known as the [^1]AA-HES, Is an advanced hazardous environment suit that can contain the entire person. 

AA-HES is designed to assist personel in tasks that are extremely hazardous, carrying or transporting heavy items, or assisting in search and rescue. 

# INSPIRATION 

- The HEV suit from half-life 2. also known as the Hazardous environment suit mark 5 



[^1]: "AA-HES" stands for "Advanced Augmented Hazardous Environment Suit"
